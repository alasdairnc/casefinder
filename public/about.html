About CaseDive

CaseDive is an AI-powered educational platform designed to help Justice Studies students, legal researchers, and educators better understand Canadian criminal law through interactive analysis and case law exploration.

⚠️ Educational Purpose Only
CaseDive is strictly an educational research tool. It does not provide legal advice, and all information should be verified with official sources. Always consult qualified legal professionals for any legal matters.

Our Mission

To democratize access to criminal law education by providing an intuitive, AI-assisted platform that makes complex legal concepts more accessible to students and researchers while maintaining the highest standards of accuracy and educational integrity.

How CaseDive Works

🔍 Intelligent Charge Analysis

Our AI analyzes scenario descriptions to identify potentially applicable Criminal Code sections, helping students understand how legal principles apply to real-world situations.

📚 Case Law Integration

Seamlessly connects to CanLII (Canadian Legal Information Institute) to provide relevant judicial decisions and precedents, showing how courts have interpreted and applied criminal law.

🎓 Educational Framework

Designed specifically for Justice Studies curricula, helping students bridge the gap between theoretical legal concepts and practical application.

✅ Citation Verification

Automatically verifies all legal citations and case references to ensure accuracy and reliability for academic research.
