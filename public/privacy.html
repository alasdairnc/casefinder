Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 15, 2026

Information We Collect

CaseDive ("we", "us", or "our") collects information you provide directly to us, such as when you use our criminal code analysis tool. This may include search queries, analysis requests, and relevance feedback or optional notes you submit about case-law results.

Search history is kept only in the active browser session and clears when you reload or close the page. We do not persist public search history in browser storage across sessions.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide our AI-powered criminal code analysis service
  • Improve our service and develop new features
  • Review user-reported irrelevant results to improve match quality
  • Ensure compliance with legal and educational standards

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except as described in this policy.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with the following third parties:

  • CanLII — Canadian Legal Information Institute, for case law data. See CanLII's privacy policy for their data practices.
  • Anthropic — We send scenario analysis requests from our server to Anthropic to generate structured legal research output.
  • Vercel Analytics — We use Vercel Analytics to understand aggregate site usage and performance.
  • Sentry — We use Sentry for error monitoring and incident investigation. Sensitive request fields are redacted before events are sent.

Cookies and Tracking

We use only the cookies or browser storage needed to operate the site and measure aggregate performance. CaseDive does not run third-party ad scripts or advertising cookies.

Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for educational purposes and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
